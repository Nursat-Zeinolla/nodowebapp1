MySchedule

Welcome to MySchedule homepage!
The main purpose of this website is to help people organize their time-management. You will be able to make your daily schedule. Therefore, you will be not be afraid that you will forget to execute important tasks. However, you should sign in prior to make a schedule or sign up, if you are a new user.

The server side of this website was built with pure Node.js, Express, and MongoDB.
Meanwhile, the frontend is just HTML and CSS.
